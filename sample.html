About.

Karwar is a city which is popularly known as Kashmir of Karnataka. It is the main city of Uttar Kannada district and it is also administrative center of a district. More than its administrative power this city is recognized for its beautiful nature. Karwar is a city which contains mixed habitat of nature. On one side we can see a coastal region with the dense mangrove forest on the other hand we can also see a green treasure of nature that is Western Ghats, Western Ghat consists of dense forest of evergreen forest which is also known as Sahyadri ghats.

Local residents of Karwar are known as karwarian. We can see a cultural diversity in this city people from different caste, religion live hormonally among themselves. Basically people are calm, silent and peace lover so in order to motivate such youth and to guide them kadalsiri youth club was started in 2019 July.

Overall this youth club has been working selflessly and with good motto of social service from its initial days to present and it wishes to do better and greater works in future.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF KADALSIRI

➢ Social services is a main motto.

➢ To guide youth in a good way and to build a healthy society .

➢ Protection of environment.

➢ To create educational and social awareness.

➢ To encourage people towards Sports, yoga and other healthy physical activities.

➢ Encouraging to develop cultural diversity.

➢ To offer volunteer service during natural disaster and emergency situation.

➢ To encourage other NGOs and social clubs.

➢ To contribute in fulfilling the dream of Swachh Bharat.

➢ To shape the youths into responsible citizens.
