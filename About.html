Karwar is a city which is popularly known as Kashmir of Karnataka. It is the main city of Uttar Kannada district and it is also administrative center of a district. More than its administrative power this city is recognized for its beautiful nature. Karwar is a city which contains mixed habitat of nature. On one side we can see a coastal region with the dense mangrove forest on the other hand we can also see a green treasure of nature that is Western Ghats, Western Ghat consists of dense forest of evergreen forest which is also known as Sahyadri ghats. Local residents of Karwar are known as karwarian. We can see a cultural diversity in this city people from different caste, religion live hormonally among themselves. Basically people are calm, silent and peace lover so in order to motivate such youth and to guide them kadalsiri youth club was started in 2019 July.
